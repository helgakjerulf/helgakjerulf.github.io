Neptún magazine

2018

Neptún is an Iceland-based magazine concerned with all things creative. It is published in print, approximately once or twice a year. Although artists from around the world are regularly featured on its pages, Neptún’s main concern is the Icelandic local scene. Thus, Neptún aims to serve as a platform for young artists and to support Icelandic creatives.
